### Tweet 2 📋

What is MiniQueue?

It's a simple job queue as a service. You create a queue → push jobs → workers pick them up. That's it.

Built for devs who need queues without the DevOps headache.

Think: "Twilio for job queues" but simpler.

---

### Tweet 3 🤔

Why we built it:

Every project at some point needs a queue — email sending, image processing, webhooks, background tasks.

But setting up Redis+Bull for a simple queue is overkill. You spend more time configuring than coding.

MiniQueue = one `curl` and you're done.
